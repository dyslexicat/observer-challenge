"The sky above the port was the color of television, tuned to a dead channel." Neuromancer - William Gibson
 Being the security officer of your Hack Club isn't always the most exciting job but today is different. You wake up and the first thing you see is your Slack notifications going crazy. Apparently there has been a heist and everyone's @banker accounts are emptied out.

You look at @banker's transaction logs and see that all the money has been transferred to @aeon and @zeno's accounts. You decide to look through the Slack logs and see that they used a private messaging website to plan the heist. Upon visiting the link, you realize you have access to their private chatroom since the website is poorly secured. You start going through the messages and evidently they planned this as a challenge for you. You briefly get a glimpse of the code you need to bring everything back to normal but it vanishes before your eyes.
